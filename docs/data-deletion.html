Data Deletion Request

Last updated: April 8, 2024

Important: Please read this information carefully before proceeding with your data deletion request.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Your account information
  • App usage data
  • Device information
  • Technical logs and crash reports
  • Any stored preferences or settings

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please use the form below or follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@aething.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include your registered email address
  3. Specify which data you want to delete (all data or specific types)
  4. Provide any additional information that might help us identify your account

Quick Deletion Request Form

Processing Time

We will process your request within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

What Happens After Deletion

  • Your account will be permanently deactivated
  • All associated data will be permanently deleted from our servers
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to use the app again
  • Some data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes
